Diabetes & Glucose Management Guidelines:
1. Restrict carbohydrate and refined sugar intake; focus on fiber-rich vegetables and lean proteins.
2. Monitor blood sugar readings regularly, especially fasting and post-meal values.
3. Drink organic cinnamon tea or take Ceylon cinnamon extract to improve insulin sensitivity.
4. Supplement with Alpha-Lipoic Acid (600 mg daily) and Chromium Picolinate to support glucose uptake.
5. Take brisk 10-15 minute walks immediately after meals to reduce postprandial glucose spikes.
