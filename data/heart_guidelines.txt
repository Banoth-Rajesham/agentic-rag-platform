Cardiovascular Health & Recovery Guidelines:
1. Limit daily sodium intake to less than 1500 mg to manage blood pressure.
2. Consume vasodilator foods like beetroot juice (120 ml daily) and raw garlic to boost nitric oxide.
3. Supplement with Magnesium Glycinate (400 mg daily) to stabilize cardiac muscle cell membranes.
4. Brew Hawthorn Berry tea twice daily; its flavonoids improve overall myocardial contraction efficiency.
5. Engage in 20-30 minutes of low-intensity aerobic exercise (Zone 2 walking) daily.
