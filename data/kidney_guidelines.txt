Kidney Health & Recovery Guidelines:
1. Stay hyper-hydrated by drinking at least 3.0 to 3.5 Liters of filtered water daily.
2. Consume fresh lemon juice or organic citric acid, which binds to calcium and inhibits stone formation.
3. Limit dietary oxalates (spinach, beets, nuts, chocolate) to prevent calcium oxalate crystallization.
4. Reduce sodium and animal protein intake to lower filtration workload and normalize blood urea nitrogen (BUN).
5. Drink kidney-cleansing herbal teas like dandelion root, nettle leaf, or corn silk tea twice daily.
