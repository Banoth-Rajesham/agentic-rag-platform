Thyroid Health & Recovery Guidelines:
1. Ensure adequate iodine, selenium, and zinc intake to support thyroid hormone synthesis.
2. Limit raw goitrogenic foods (such as uncooked cabbage, kale, and soy) if thyroid activity is low.
3. Manage stress through adaptogens like Ashwagandha (500 mg daily) to optimize cortisol and T4-to-T3 conversion.
4. Consult with an endocrinologist for custom hormone replacement therapy if TSH remains elevated.
5. Get daily morning sunlight to support Vitamin D synthesis, which is crucial for autoimmune thyroid health.
